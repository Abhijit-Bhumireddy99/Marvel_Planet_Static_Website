Captain America
Steve Rogers

The First Avenger.

During World War II, the nationalistic Steve Rogers was offered a place in the military's top operation: Rebirth. Injected with an experimental super-serum, Steve Rogers advanced from the treatment with enhanced endurance, strength, and reaction time. With an immensive training and an indestructible Vibranium shield, In a short time, Steve Rogers became the country's greatest weapon: CAPTAIN AMERICA! During the final days of World War II, Steve Rogers was frozen in ice for nearly 70 years and then discovered and revived decades later. Now the living legend continues the war against bad guys in modern times as a member and leader of
The Avengers!
