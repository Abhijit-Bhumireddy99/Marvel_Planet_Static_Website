One can say whether a movie is a hit or an avg or a flop at the box office by looking into a movie's box office figures. Marvel has been a hit at the box office for over a decade.                Marvel TV Shows : Is there a Box Office for TV Shows?

There is no separate box office for Marvel TV Shows, as they directly released on OTT platforms. The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) television series are American superhero TV shows based on Marvel Comic characters. The TV shows have been in production since 2013. Unlike Movies, TV shows success rates are measured by audiences views. Marvel Studios is creating its own Marvel TV shows that will be basically tied to what’s happening in the MCU movies and will be exclusively streaming on Disney Plus.
