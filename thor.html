Thor
Thor Odinson

God of Thunder.  The Strongest Avenger.

The son of Odin, the prince to the throne of Asgard - Thor, renowned as the mightiest hero of mythology! The mighty Thor controls an enchanted Uru hammer (Mjolnir), and he is the master of thunder and lightning. Thor uses his mighty abilities as "The God of Thunder" to protect his home Asgard and planet Earth. Thor is a member of The Avengers.
