Iron Man
Tony Stark

Genius. Billionaire. Philanthropist.

The Invincible.

Tony Stark A.K.A Iron Man, is a billionaire and mastermind superhero from the Marvel Universe. Tony Stark was the arrogant son of wealthy, weapon manufacturer Howard Stark. Tony cared only about himself, but he would have a change of heart after he was kidnapped by terrorists and badly injured. Terrorists forced him to create a weapon of apocalypse, Stark instead created a suit of armor powerful enough for him to escape. Tony uses his enormous resources and intellect to make the world a better place as "The Invincible Iron Man". Tony Stark is a member of The Avengers.
